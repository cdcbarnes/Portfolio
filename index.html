About Me

I'm Clayton Barnes, and this is my project page! The intent of this page is to give you an idea of what I'm capable of with regards to my coding prowess, but first, perhaps a little bit about me!

I am a 40 year old husband and father of 3 children, 2 boys and 1 girl. I have been obsessed with computers since I got my first one when I was in Grade 6, and built my first websites by Grade 7, when Netscape Navigator was still the browser of choice, and the coolest thing on the World Wide Web was frames and animated gifs.

I grew up on mIRC and MySpace, ICQ and AOL Instant Messenger, and have watched the World Wide Web "grow up" so to speak, and while I've always wanted to get involved, life kept getting in my way. After 12 years in the Canadian Forces, followed by an additional 12 in the Oil Industry, I feel like I finally need to give in to my true passion, and dive into the world of coding.
